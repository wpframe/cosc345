Education and Career

Welcome to the Education and Career simulation!

Here's how it works:

  1. Pick a University: You can choose a university to attend - the more money you have in total balance, the higher the chance of selection.
  2. Tuition: During your education, you'll have to pay tuition over a number of years.
  3. Salary: After completing your education, you'll be given a starting salary based on your choices.
  4. Time Progression: Time will move forward, and you can request raises to increase your salary.

Enjoy your journey towards a successful career!
